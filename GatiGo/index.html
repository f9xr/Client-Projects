Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How are drivers verified?

Every driver undergoes a strict multi-layer verification process, including government ID vetting, vehicle registration checks, and a pristine driving history requirement.

Can I schedule recurring rides?

Yes. Our app allows you to set up persistent schedules (like a Monday-Friday office commute) so you are automatically matched with your preferred network.

How do payments work?

It's entirely cashless. You link your preferred payment method (UPI, Cards, or Wallet). The ride cost is automatically split and deducted once the trip completes.
